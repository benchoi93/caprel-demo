The base policy is a soft-greedy toward the nearest corner (proxy for a trained GFN). CapRel adds a state-visit-count penalty that reallocates probability from over-visited children to under-visited siblings.
Implementation: single HTML file, ~250 LoC of vanilla JS. All sampling runs client-side. MDP: 10×10 grid, actions = {up, right, stop}, trajectory ends on stop or at boundary.
